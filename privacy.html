  • To create and authenticate your account
  • To provide the warranty tracking functionality

We do NOT use your data for: - Advertising or third-party marketing - Selling your data - Analyzing your warranty content without your explicit request (AI features only activate when you tap the corresponding button)

Data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure (Firestore database, Firebase Storage for files), in the European server region (eur3). Firebase complies with international security standards; see their privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
